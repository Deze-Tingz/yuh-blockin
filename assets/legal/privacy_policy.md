# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 10, 2024**

DezeTingz ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Yuh Blockin' mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Yuh Blockin', you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we generate a unique user identifier to associate your data with your account.
- **License Plate Data**: Vehicle license plate numbers you choose to monitor within the App.
- **Subscription Information**: Purchase and subscription status for premium features.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Tokens**: We collect Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to send you push notifications about vehicle alerts.
- **Device Information**: Basic device information necessary for app functionality and push notification delivery (device type, operating system).

### 1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Location data
- Personal identification documents
- Payment card details (handled securely by third-party payment processors)
- Contacts or address book data
- Photos or media (unless explicitly provided by you)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
- Send push notifications for vehicle alerts you have configured
- Process subscriptions and manage premium features
- Improve and optimize the App experience
- Communicate with you regarding updates or support inquiries
- Comply with legal obligations

- Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase with encryption at rest and in transit.
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.
- While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

Upon account deletion:
- Your user data will be permanently removed from our servers
- License plate monitoring data will be deleted
- Push notification tokens will be invalidated
